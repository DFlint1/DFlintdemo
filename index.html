I was raised in Texas, where I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from the University of Texas at Austin, completing my degree in just 2.5 years. My professional career launched at Lockheed Martin Information Management Systems, where I had the opportunity to lead a team of 50, cultivating a deep appreciation for teamwork, collaboration, and leadership. This experience not only solidified my ability to guide teams but also underscored the transformative power of effective communication. For more than two decades, I devoted myself to the home-building industry in the Austin area. As a co-founder of a startup, I gained extensive expertise in office development, management, staff training, project oversight, marketing, and the legal and financial aspects of running a business. In my role as VP of Operations at Flint Estate Builders, I led the IT, finance, and marketing departments, working closely with a versatile team of subcontractors. This entrepreneurial path sharpened my adaptability and resilience, qualities that are crucial to navigating the complexities of any business environment. Despite my strong background in project management, home building, and finance, my passion for technology—especially web applications—has been a consistent thread throughout my career. I’ve designed and refined websites for both personal and professional use, continually exploring tools such as AutoCAD, accounting software, design platforms, and social media to drive creativity and business growth. This passion for technology has seamlessly integrated with my professional journey, allowing me to tackle challenges with fresh, innovative solutions. As I transition fully into web development, I am energized by the opportunity to create impactful, forward-thinking applications and contribute to meaningful projects. I am eager to leverage my diverse experience and skill set to drive success in the web development space. I welcome the chance to connect and discuss how my unique background and passion for innovation can contribute to the success of your organization. Together, we can build transformative solutions that drive growth and progress.

- Dana Flint
